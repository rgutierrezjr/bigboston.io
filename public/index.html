our process

Our process incorporates years of experience in gathering and analyzing our customer's unique requirements to design and develop a finished product that meets their needs.

01

Requirement Gathering & Analysis

You tell us about your unique problem, we listen and then formulate a set of requirements based on your needs. We then take those requirements, analyze them, and present solutions for you. You'll have plenty of options to choose from. We'll even let you know if we're not the best option for you.

02

Approval & Design

Once you approve one of our proposals we'll get started on the designs of your selected solution. You'll be with us every step of the way to avoid any surprises during the development and deliverable processes.

03

Development

With the proposal & designs as our guide, our development team begins work on your solution with state of the art tools and technologies. Again, we'll periodically check-in with the state of your project to maintain transparency and honesty.

04

Delivery & Deployment

After unit and user acceptance testing, we'll present your finished project to you. Upon your approval, we'll launch it for immediate use.

what we offer.

website development

Do you need a website developed from the ground up and don't know where to start? Do you have an existing application and need a new feature implemented? We can help.

multi-industry experience

We know how important it is for you to have a modern and stylish solution, but we also know it's even more important you have one that's intuitive, responsive, and effective for your operation. We'll use our multi-industry domain experience to get you just that.

continuous support

We understand maintaining a web application is a continuous endeavor. Let us host your project through Heroku and we can resolve your issues, update content, and implement & deploy updates on the fly.

transparency

No one likes being left in the dark. We'll keep you informed every step of the way. Whether it's communicating milestones, requesting requirement clarification, or alerting you to a possible delay. The best solutions are made when we work together.
